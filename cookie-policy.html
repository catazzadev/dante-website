1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

Similar technologies such as pixels, local storage, and SDKs may also be used in our mobile App. This policy covers all such technologies collectively referred to as "cookies".

2. How We Use Cookies

Danteapp.ai and our website at www.danteapp.ai use cookies for the following purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website and App to function. They enable core features such as page navigation, access to secure areas, and account authentication. Without these cookies, the service cannot operate properly.

Cookie / Technology Purpose Duration
Session token Keeps you logged in during your visit Session / up to 30 days
Authentication token Verifies your identity after login Up to 30 days

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics tools to understand how users interact with our App and website. This helps us improve the experience, fix issues, and develop new features.

  • Mixpanel — for product analytics

We do not use any advertising or marketing cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in the following ways:

4.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete individual cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set preferences for first-party vs. third-party cookies

Please note that blocking essential cookies may prevent the website from functioning correctly.

4.2 Mobile Device Settings

On mobile devices, you can manage tracking permissions through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads

4.3 Opt-Out of Analytics

You can opt out of Mixpanel tracking by visiting Mixpanel's opt-out page.
